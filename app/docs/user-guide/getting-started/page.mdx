# Getting started

Ascendio is a focused job application tracker that keeps every application, interview, and offer in one calm, organized place. This guide walks you through signing in and adding your first job.

## Prerequisites

You only need one thing to use Ascendio:

- A **Google account** — Ascendio uses Google OAuth for authentication. No password, no email verification, no forms.

## Signing in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to ascendio.app">
    Open your browser and navigate to [ascendio.app](https://ascendio.app). You will land on the homepage.

    <ImageCaption
      src="/docs/getting-started/landing-page.png"
      alt="Ascendio landing page"
      caption="The Ascendio landing page"
    />

  </Step>

  <Step title="Click 'Start for free'">
    Click the **Start for free** button in the hero section or the **Get started** button in the navbar. Both take you to the sign-in page.

    <ImageCaption
      src="/docs/getting-started/sign-in-page.png"
      alt="Ascendio sign-in page"
      caption="The sign-in page — one button, no forms"
    />

  </Step>

  <Step title="Continue with Google">
    Click **Continue with Google**. A Google OAuth popup will appear. Select your Google account and grant the requested permissions.

    <Callout type="info">
      Ascendio only receives your name, email address, and profile photo from Google. We never see your Google password.
    </Callout>

  </Step>

  <Step title="You're in">
    After signing in you are redirected to your dashboard. If this is your first time, you will see an empty Kanban board ready for your first job.

    <ImageCaption
      src="/docs/getting-started/empty-dashboard.png"
      alt="Empty Ascendio dashboard"
      caption="Your dashboard on first sign-in — empty and ready"
    />

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Adding your first job

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Add job'">
    On the dashboard, click the **Add job** button. A form will appear asking for the basic details of the position.

    <ImageCaption
      src="/docs/getting-started/add-job-button.png"
      alt="Add job button on the dashboard"
      caption="The Add job button on the Kanban board"
    />

  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in the details">
    Enter the company name, job title, and any other details you have. At minimum, a company name and job title are required.

    <ImageCaption
      src="/docs/getting-started/add-job-form.png"
      alt="Add job form"
      caption="The add job form — fill in what you know, edit the rest later"
    />

    <Callout type="tip">
      Don't worry about filling everything in right away. You can always come back and add more details as you progress through the application process.
    </Callout>

  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a stage">
    Select the stage that best describes where this job currently is. If you haven't applied yet, choose **Wishlist**. If you've already applied, choose **Applied**.

    <ImageCaption
      src="/docs/getting-started/stage-selector.png"
      alt="Stage selector in the add job form"
      caption="Pick the stage that matches where you are right now"
    />

  </Step>

  <Step title="Save the job">
    Click **Save**. The job card will appear on your Kanban board in the stage you selected. You're all set.

    <ImageCaption
      src="/docs/getting-started/job-card-added.png"
      alt="Job card added to the Kanban board"
      caption="Your first job card on the board"
    />

  </Step>
</Steps>

As a job progresses, move it forward by dragging the card to the next stage or by opening the job and changing the stage from within. Every stage unlocks a dedicated set of fields — interview notes in **Interviewing**, compensation breakdown in **Offer**, and reflection notes in **Rejected**.

<Callout type="info">
  When you move a job to a new stage, all previous stage data stays accessible
  in collapsed sections at the bottom of the job panel. Nothing is lost.
</Callout>
