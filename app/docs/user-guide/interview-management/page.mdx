# Interview management

When a job moves into the **Interviewing** stage, Ascendio gives you a dedicated space to prepare for and track every round. The page is split into two parts — a global **Interview Prep** section at the top, and an **Interview Rounds** section below where each individual interview is logged.

<ImageCaption
  src="/docs/interview-management/overview.png"
  alt="Interview management overview"
  caption="The Interviewing stage — prep at the top, rounds below"
/>

## Interview prep

The Interview Prep section is global — it applies to the entire interview process for this job, not to any single round. It contains four fields:

## Interview rounds

Below the prep section, the **Your Interviews** area lets you log each individual interview round as a separate card. Each card shows the interview type, date, duration, interviewer, outcome, and rating at a glance.

<ImageCaption
  src="/docs/interview-management/rounds.png"
  alt="Interview rounds"
  caption="Interview rounds — each round is a separate numbered card"
/>

### Adding an interview

Click **Add Interview** to open the interview sheet. Fill in the details for the round and click **Add Interview** to save it.

<ImageCaption
  src="/docs/interview-management/add-interview-sheet.png"

  caption="The Add Interview sheet — slide in from the right"
/>

### Editing an interview

Click any existing interview card to open the same sheet in edit mode. Make your changes and click **Save** to update the round.

## Previous stage notes

Below the interview rounds, Ascendio preserves your **Applied** notes and **Wishlist** research from earlier stages. These are read-only and serve as context — a reminder of why you applied and what you noted during the application process.

<Callout type="info">
  Previous stage notes are never deleted when a job moves forward. They are
  always accessible at the bottom of the current stage panel.
</Callout>
