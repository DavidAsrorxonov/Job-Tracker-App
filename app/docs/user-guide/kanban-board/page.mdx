# Kanban board

The Kanban board is the heart of Ascendio. Every job you track lives here, organized into five columns that represent the stages of a job search. At a glance you can see exactly where every opportunity stands.

## Job cards

Each job on the board is represented by a card. The card shows the most important information at a glance without needing to open the job:

- **Company name** — the organization you applied to
- **Job title** — the position you are pursuing
- **Location** — where the role is based
- **Salary** — the compensation range if you have recorded it
- **Tags** — any custom labels you have added
- **Applied at** — the date you submitted your application
- **Last updated** — when the job was last modified

## Opening a job

Click any job card to open its full detail page at `/dashboard/[id]`. This is where you can view and edit everything about the job — notes, contacts, documents, interview rounds, offer details, and the full stage history.

<ImageCaption
  src="/docs/kanban-board/images/job-detail.png"
  alt="Job detail page"
  caption="The job detail page — click any card to open it"
/>

The detail page shows:

- **Job title and company** — always visible at the top
- **Location, salary, and tags** — editable at any time
- **Applied at and last updated** — automatically tracked
- **Current stage** — shown clearly with the option to change it
- **Stage-specific panels** — each stage has its own dedicated section for relevant information

<Callout type="info">
  The URL for each job is unique — you can bookmark a specific job and return to
  it directly at any time.
</Callout>

## Adding a new job

To add a job to the board, click the **Add job** button on the dashboard. The job will appear in whichever stage you select during creation.

For a full walkthrough of adding your first job, see the [Getting started](/docs/user-guide/getting-started) guide.
