# Offer evaluation

When a job moves into the **Offer** stage, Ascendio gives you a structured space to evaluate the offer in full — compensation breakdown, offer details, pros and cons, and a final decision. The goal is to make a clear-headed decision with everything visible in one place.

<ImageCaption
  src="/docs/offer-evaluation/overview.png"
  alt="Offer evaluation overview"
  caption="The Offer stage — compensation, details, pros and cons, and your decision"
/>

## Pros & cons

The Pros & Cons section helps you think through the offer before committing. Add as many pros and cons as you need — one per entry.

<ImageCaption
  src="/docs/offer-evaluation/pros-cons.png"
  alt="Pros and cons section"
  caption="Weigh the offer before making your decision"
/>

Type a pro or con in the input field and press **+** to add it to the list. You can add and remove entries at any time before saving.

<Callout type="tip">
  Be honest with yourself here. A long pros list does not automatically mean you
  should accept — a single dealbreaker con can outweigh everything else.
</Callout>
